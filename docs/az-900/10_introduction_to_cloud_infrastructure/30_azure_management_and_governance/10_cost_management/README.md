## 📚 What You Should Know for the Exam

After this section, you should be able to:

- identify factors that influence Azure costs
- understand the purpose of the **Pricing Calculator**
- recognize how **Microsoft Cost Management** helps track spending
- explain how **tags** support organization and cost reporting
- describe basic cost optimization strategies

---

## 🧠 Exam Focus Areas

Pay special attention to:

- **pay-as-you-go pricing**
- **estimating costs before deployment**
- **monitoring current spending**
- **resource tagging** for organization and reporting
- **cost optimization** through right-sizing and cleanup

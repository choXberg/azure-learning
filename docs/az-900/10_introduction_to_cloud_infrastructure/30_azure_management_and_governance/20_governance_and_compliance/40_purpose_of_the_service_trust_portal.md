# Service Trust Portal – Summary

## Overview
The **Microsoft Service Trust Portal (STP)** is a platform that provides access to information about:

- Security
- Privacy
- Compliance

It helps organizations understand how Microsoft protects cloud services and customer data.

---

## Purpose

The Service Trust Portal allows you to:

- Review Microsoft’s **security and compliance practices**
- Access **audit reports and certifications**
- Understand how Microsoft implements **controls and processes**
- Support **regulatory and compliance requirements**

---

## Access Requirements

- Available at: https://servicetrust.microsoft.com/
- Requires:
  - A Microsoft cloud account (Microsoft Entra ID – work or school account)
  - Acceptance of a **non-disclosure agreement (NDA)** for some documents

---

## Key Features

### 1. Service Trust Portal Home
- Entry point to all resources and information

---

### 2. My Library
- Save (pin) documents for quick access
- Receive **notifications** when documents are updated

---

### 3. All Documents
- Central repository for all available documents
- Allows browsing and pinning documents to My Library

---

## Content Available

The portal provides:

- Compliance reports
- Audit documentation
- Security whitepapers
- Certifications (e.g., ISO, SOC)

> Note: Documents are typically available for at least 12 months or until updated versions are released.

---

## Key Takeaways (Exam Focus)

- Service Trust Portal = **Transparency for security, privacy, and compliance**
- Provides:
  - Audit reports
  - Certifications
  - Security documentation
- Requires:
  - Authenticated Microsoft account
  - NDA for some materials
- Helps organizations meet **compliance and regulatory requirements**
